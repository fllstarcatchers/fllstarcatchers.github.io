Introducing team Star Catchers! We present to you a game-changing solution through our innovation project: a hand brace/stabilizer designed specifically for viola and violin players. Our invention guarantees that these young artists can follow their interest in music without worrying about the possibility of developing Carpal Tunnel Syndrome. Our product offers the crucial support and stability many need when beginning their musical journey. By eliminating the improper movement in your left wrist and hand, our device will prevent the formation of early bad habits. Our goal in producing this website is to raise awareness of the significance of good posture.
